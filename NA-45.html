NA-45


The NA-45 is unlocked at level 26. The weapon uses exploding rounds: the first shot acts as a tracker, and when the second round is shot nearby, they explode. Note that this tracking and exploding sequence of shots will not work if the shots are fired too far apart, in which case they act as regular sniper rounds.
