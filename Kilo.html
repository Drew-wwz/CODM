KILO BOLT-ACTION


Kilo Bolt-Action is in marksman class of COD Mobile that has high damage, good accuracy, and can 1 shot enemy in close to medium range when hitting the upper body area. It is the first sniper that can use ironsight. You need to reach tier 21 in Season 9 Battle Pass to unlock Kilo Bolt-Action.
