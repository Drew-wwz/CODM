FENNEC


Fennec is still one of the top weapons in MP, even after the nerf of the Akimbo perk. It deals decent damage in close range, which can 5 shots kill up to 11 meters, and 6 shots kill up to 16 meters. To rank this weapon, it has low damage, fairly good accuracy, and great weapon mobility.
