AK-47


The AK-47 is in the single player mode and is the weapon most frequently used by the in-game enemies. The AK-47 is one of the first assault rifles unlocked in multiplayer, being available as soon as Create-a-Class is unlocked. It is a high-damage, slow-firing assault rifle.
