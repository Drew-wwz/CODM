M21-EBR


The M21 EBR is the lowest damage sniper rifle in its class. By default, the M21 EBR can only achieve a one shot kill to the head. An M21 EBR equipped with a Silencer cannot achieve a one shot kill at all. An M21 EBR with Stopping Power can achieve a one shot kill to the head, neck, and upper torso.
