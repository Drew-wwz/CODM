DL-Q33


The DL Q33 is rather similar to the Intervention from previous games, in terms of appearance and behavior. It has great dynamics: good sprint speed, good ADS speed, and a huge hit box multiplier. Being able to one-shot kill when hitting the belly, this sniper rifle is one of the best choices for going close range, and is extremely forgiving at longer ranges.
